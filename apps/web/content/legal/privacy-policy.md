This Privacy Policy describes how SmartBeak collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the SmartBeak website, applications, APIs, and related services.

By using SmartBeak, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:

- account information such as name, email address, organization details, and authentication identifiers
- billing and transaction information processed through our payment providers
- content and files you upload, create, publish, or connect to the service
- usage data such as page views, feature interactions, device data, browser data, IP address, and approximate location derived from network information
- operational data such as logs, error reports, audit events, and security telemetry
- information from connected third-party services when you choose to integrate them

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide, operate, and maintain the service
- authenticate users and secure accounts
- process transactions and manage subscriptions
- support publishing, analytics, AI, storage, and workflow features
- prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access
- improve product performance, reliability, and user experience
- communicate with you about service activity, updates, support, and billing
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## Customer Content and AI Processing

Customer content may be processed to provide hosting, search, analytics, media handling, workflow execution, and AI-assisted features.

Where SmartBeak uses third-party AI or infrastructure providers to deliver requested functionality, relevant content may be transmitted to those providers strictly for service operation.

You are responsible for ensuring that your use of SmartBeak and any data you submit is lawful and appropriate for the sensitivity of the information involved.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

SmartBeak may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, measure usage, and support site functionality.

If cookies that are not strictly necessary are used, SmartBeak may provide controls to manage consent where required by law.

## When We Share Information

We may share information:

- with service providers and subprocessors that help us operate the platform
- with payment processors to complete transactions
- with hosting, storage, analytics, communication, and AI vendors acting on our behalf
- within your organization workspace according to your role and configuration
- when required by law, regulation, court order, or valid legal process
- to protect rights, safety, security, and the integrity of the service
- in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale

We do not sell personal information in exchange for monetary compensation.

## Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain security and auditability.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data, workspace settings, legal requirements, and whether an account remains active.

## Security

SmartBeak uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## International Processing

Your information may be processed in jurisdictions other than your own, including locations where SmartBeak or its service providers operate infrastructure.

When required, SmartBeak will rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms for cross-border data handling.

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information.

You may also be able to update account information directly through your workspace settings. For privacy-related requests, use the contact channels published on the SmartBeak website or within your account workspace.

## Children's Privacy

SmartBeak is not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

## Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above indicates when this version became effective. Your continued use of SmartBeak after an update becomes effective means the updated policy applies to your use of the service.
